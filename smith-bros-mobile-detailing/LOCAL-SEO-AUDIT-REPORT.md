# Local SEO & Google Maps Audit Report
## Smith Bros Mobile Detailing (sbmobiledetailing.com)
### Audit Date: May 14, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

Smith Bros Mobile Detailing has a strong reputation (5.0 stars, 545 combined Google reviews across 3 listings, 481 Yelp reviews) and a well-built website with 30+ location pages per service. However, they are **critically underperforming in Google Maps** — the primary traffic driver for local service businesses.

### The 3 Biggest Issues Found

1. **THREE DUPLICATE GBP LISTINGS (Critical)** — Smith Bros has THREE separate Google Business Profile listings splitting 545 reviews and confusing Google:
   - **Listing A (Storefront):** "Smith Bros Mobile Detailing" — 233 reviews, has physical address, secondary categories, active Google Posts (11 hours ago), owner review responses, photos — **this is the well-managed primary listing** that appears in local pack
   - **Listing B (Service Area):** "Smith Bros Mobile Detailing, Ceramic, Tint, & PPF" — 275 reviews, NO physical address, also has active posts and review responses — **but cannot appear in local pack** because it has no address. Engagement effort here is wasted for Maps ranking purposes
   - **Listing C (Abandoned):** "Smith Bros Mobile Detailing" — 37 reviews, NO physical address, reviews are 3-4 years old with no responses — dead weight
   - **The three-way review split is the core problem.** 545 reviews at 5.0 stars on ONE listing would make Smith Bros the highest-reviewed mobile detailer in North County. Instead, Listing A shows only 233 reviews while competitors show 437 (Blueberry) or 381 (Marco's). Listings B and C should be merged into A or deleted.

2. **Duplicate Listings Diluting Authority** — While Listing A is well-optimized with secondary categories, business description, and services populated, the existence of two additional duplicate listings (B and C) is splitting 545 reviews three ways and may be confusing Google's algorithms about which listing to surface in local pack results.

3. **No Website Schema Markup** — The website has zero structured data (JSON-LD). Competitors have comprehensive LocalBusiness, AggregateRating, FAQPage, and Service schemas that help them appear in rich results.

### Impact

Smith Bros is **invisible in the Google Maps local pack** for their most valuable keywords across ALL of San Diego County — including their home city of San Marcos. Despite having more reviews than many competitors, businesses with as few as 14 reviews outrank them.

**Estimated monthly revenue loss:** Based on competitor traffic values, Smith Bros is likely losing $5,000-$15,000/month in potential bookings by not appearing in Google Maps for their key service areas.
